More Than Just Academics

Over 30 clubs, teams, and programmes to ignite every passion

At Busiu Secondary School, We firmly believe that education extends well beyond the classroom. Our co-curricular programme is designed to nurture leadership, creativity, teamwork, resilience, and a love of lifelong learning — the qualities that truly prepare students for the future.

From competitive sports teams to drama productions, debate competitions to community service, every student is encouraged to participate in at least two activities per term.

Sports & Athletics

Competing at District, Regional, and National levels

⚽

Football (Soccer)

Boys & Girls teams competing in the regional school leagues. Training every Tuesday & Thursday, 4–5:30pm.

🏀

Basketball

Mixed team with dedicated court. Regional champions in 2022 & 2023. Training Mon & Wed, 4–5:30pm.

🏃

Athletics & Track

Sprint, long distance, hurdles, long jump, and high jump. Districts, Regional and National championships entrants every year.

🏊

Swimming

Olympic-size pool on campus. Competitive and recreational swimming available for all year groups.

Arts, Culture & Society

Creativity, expression, and civic engagement

🎵

School Orchestra & Choir

80-member symphony orchestra and 60-voice choir. Annual concerts and national music festivals.

🎭

Drama & Theatre

Two major productions each year plus short-play workshops. Students handle all aspects including set design and lighting.

🗣️

Debate & Public Speaking

Competing in national debate leagues. Model United Nations participants since 2015. Builds critical thinking & confidence.

🤝

Community Service Club

Volunteering, fundraising, environmental campaigns, and outreach to local orphanages and elderly homes.

STEM Clubs & Technology

Hands-on exploration of science, engineering, and digital skills

🤖

Robotics Club

Build and program robots for competitions. Regional robotics champions 2022–2024. Open to all year groups.

💻

Coding & App Development

Python, JavaScript, and mobile app development workshops. Students have published apps on app stores.

🌿

Environmental Science Club

Monitoring local biodiversity, composting projects, school garden, and climate advocacy campaigns.

📷

Photography & Filmmaking

Digital photography, videography, and editing. Produces the school's annual documentary, Magazines and yearbook.

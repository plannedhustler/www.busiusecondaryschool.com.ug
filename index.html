Headteacher's Message

A Message from Our Headteacher

Welcome to Busiu secondary School — a place where young minds are inspired to dream big, think critically, and act with integrity. For nearly four decades, we have been dedicated to nurturing the full potential of every student who walks through our gate of Busiu secondary School.

Our philosophy is simple: every child is unique and talented. Our role is to provide the environment, guidance, and opportunities that allow that talent to blossom. We pair rigorous academics with a rich programme of co-curricular activities so that students develop into confident, compassionate, and capable individuals and Leaders of Tomorrow.

School Administration
The school Administration works together with qualified teaching and non-teaching staff to ensure quality services delivery and student success.

I invite you to explore our school and see for yourself the vibrant community of learners and educators we have built together.

Dr. Herbet Weboya, Ph.D. Headteacher & Team Leader

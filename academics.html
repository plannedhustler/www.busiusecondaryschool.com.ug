Our Curriculum

Aligned with the New Competency-Based Curriculum with national standards and enriched with global best practices

At Busiu Secondary School, our academic programme is designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare students for life beyond the classroom. We follow a nationally approved curriculum by Ministry Of Education and Sports while supplementing it with project-based learning, critical thinking exercises, and technology integration in learning Sessions.

Our three academic divisions — O'Level (Term 1–3), Junior Secondary (Term 1–3), and Senior Secondary (Term 1–3) — each have dedicated specialist teachers and tailored learning programmes.

What Makes Us Different from others

Our Academic Approach goes Beyond Memorisation

Project-Based Learning

Students solve real-world problems through cross-disciplinary team projects every Term.

Small Class Sizes

A maximum of 80 students per class ensures personalised attention from every teacher.

Technology Integration

1:1 devices like Laptop, Desktops, Projector, interactive whiteboards, and a Fully equiped and dedicated computer lab.

Pastoral Care

Every student has a dedicated form tutor who monitors academic progress and wellbeing.

Fully Equipped Labs

Purpose-built science, computer, and language laboratories support practical learning.

Academic Competitions

We enter District, Regional, National and international Olympiads in Maths, Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Agriculture, Language, and Robotics.

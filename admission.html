Admission Process

Simple steps to join Busiu Secondary School family

📋

1. Complete Application

Fill out the online admission form below with accurate information about the applicant and parent/guardian.

📁

2. Submit Documents

Bring certified copies of birth certificate,Result Slips of P.7,S.4 any of those, last school report, immunisation records, and two passport photos.

✍️

3. Entrance Assessment

Applicants sit a short assessment in English and Mathematics. The date is confirmed by email after document review.

🤝

4. Interview

Shortlisted applicants and parents attend a brief interview with our admissions team to discuss expectations.

✅

5. Offer & Enrolment

Successful applicants receive an offer letter. Pay the registration fee to secure your child's place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from prospective families

Where does Busiu Secondary School accept students from?
We accept students from age 15:26 01/07/2026 (Foundation Year 1) through to Year 12 (age 15–27). All prospective students complete an entrance assessment before placement.
Is there a sibling discount?
Yes. A 10% discount on tuition fees applies from the second child onwards. The discount is applied automatically upon enrolment. Please inform the admissions office if you have siblings already enrolled.
When does the new academic year begin?
The academic year begins in early January. Term dates are published on our website in September each year. The 2025–2026 school year begins on 7 January 2026.
Do you offer bursaries or financial assistance at Busiu Secondary School?
Yes. A limited number of need-based bursaries are available each year covering up to 50% of tuition fees. Bursary applications are assessed separately. Contact the admissions office for details and deadlines.
What Academic documents do I need to bring at Busiu Secondary School?
You will need: (1) Birth certificate (certified copy), (2) Most recent school report or academic record, (3) Immunisation / vaccination records, (4) Two recent passport-size photographs, and (5) Parent/guardian's ID document.
Can my child join Mid-term?
Mid-term placements are considered on a case-by-case basis depending on space availability and academic readiness. Please contact the admissions office directly to discuss a mid-term application.

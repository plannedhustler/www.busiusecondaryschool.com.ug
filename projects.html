Our Project Culture

Every student completes at least one major project per year

Our project-based learning approach challenges students to apply classroom knowledge to tackle genuine community and global challenges. Each project is mentored by a subject teacher and culminates in a presentation to a panel of the Teaching staff and invited community guests.

Projects span STEM, humanities, environmental science, social entrepreneurship, and the arts. The best projects are entered into District, Regional and National competitions — and we have an impressive track record of winning.
